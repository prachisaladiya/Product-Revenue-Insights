# Product Category Performance & Revenue Insights

## Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing sales data to understand revenue performance across different product categories.  
The goal was to identify top-performing categories, uncover dependencies, and highlight areas for business improvement.

---

## Objectives
- Analyze overall revenue performance  
- Identify top and low performing product categories  
- Understand revenue distribution across categories  
- Provide actionable insights to improve business growth  

---

## Tools Used
- **SQL** – Data analysis and querying  
- **Power BI** – Data visualization and dashboard creation  

---

## Key Analysis Performed
- Total revenue and order analysis  
- Monthly revenue trend analysis  
- Revenue contribution by product category  
- Funnel analysis for high-value orders  
- Cohort analysis for customer retention  

---

## Key Insights
- Electronics contributed **665K (~82%)** of total revenue  
- Furniture contributed **108K (~13%)**  
- Accessories contributed **31K (~4%)**  
- Business is highly dependent on a single category (Electronics)  
- Around **57% of orders are high-value**, indicating strong conversion  
- Customer retention is low (~20–30%), especially for new users  

---

## Business Recommendations
- Reduce dependency on Electronics by improving other categories  
- Run targeted campaigns for Furniture and Accessories  
- Improve customer retention through engagement strategies  
- Replicate strategies from high-performing periods to stabilize revenue  

---

## Dashboard Features
- Revenue trend over time  
- Category-wise revenue distribution  
- Funnel analysis  
- Cohort retention analysis  

---

## Conclusion
This project highlights key revenue drivers and risks in category dependency.  
It provides actionable insights to improve customer retention and achieve more stable business growth.
